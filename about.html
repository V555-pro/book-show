About The Authors

Samuel George
@samuelgeorge76
Samuel George is the Global Markets & Digital Advisor for the Bertelsmann Foundation. His work includes written analysis and documentary films focusing on the intersection of economics, politics, the digital revolution, and daily life. George co-authored and edited both The No Collar Economy and Our Digital World.
Felipe Buitrago
@samuelgeorge76
Coauthor of The No Collar Economy and contributor to Our Digital World. He is also the author of The Orange Economy, and currently, Colombian vice minister of Creativity Economy (his contributions do not represent in any way an official opinion of the Government of Colombia).
